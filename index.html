Services

Parking Services:

AAR Services delivers top-notch parking management solutions designed to optimize space and streamline operations. Our expert team ensures hassle-free parking experiences with efficient space utilization and professional valet services. Enhance your facility’s convenience, safety, and visitor satisfaction with our comprehensive parking solutions, tailored for shopping malls, corporate offices, hospitals, and more.

Manpower Services:

Boost operational efficiency with our skilled manpower services. We provide trained personnel for security, housekeeping, and maintenance, ensuring seamless facility management. Enhance your business operations with reliable support tailored to meet diverse industry needs.

Boom Barrier Solutions:

Upgrade your facility's security with our advanced boom barrier solutions. Our automated systems offer robust access control and streamline entry points, providing a secure, efficient way to manage vehicle flow in high-traffic areas.

Sliding Gate Motors:

Ensure secure and convenient access with our premium sliding gate motors. Designed for both commercial and residential properties, our solutions offer reliable and smooth operation, enhancing security and ease of use.

Parking Software:

Revolutionize parking management with our advanced parking software solutions. Featuring FASTag integration, our technology offers seamless, automated transactions and real-time monitoring, optimizing your parking operations for efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Road Safety Equipment:

Prioritize safety with our high-quality road safety equipment, including speed breakers, reflective markers, and signage. Designed to enhance traffic management, our solutions keep your parking areas safe and well-regulated for all users.
