Services

On-Site Parking Management

VALET PARKING SERVICES: Providing valet attendants to park vehicles for customers at venues like malls, hotels, hospitals, or event spaces.

Attendant Parking: Deploying parking attendants to manage parking lots, assist drivers, and ensure efficient space usage.

Self-Parking Management: Setting up a structured and managed self-parking system with clear signage, guidance, and security measures.

Automated Parking Systems

SMART PARKING SOLUTIONS: Integration of automated parking systems like boom barriers, parking ticket dispensers, and sensors for space detection.

Parking Guidance Systems: Real-time indicators and displays showing available parking spots, reducing search time and improving flow.

Automated Payment Systems: Contactless and digital payment options, including QR codes, mobile apps, and card payments.

Technology-Driven Solutions

PARKING MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE: Offering software for managing ticketing, billing, monitoring vehicles, and generating real-time reports.

License Plate Recognition (LPR): Using technology to scan and record license plates for seamless entry and exit, often in automated parking facilities.

Mobile App Integration: Providing apps for customers to book parking spots, make payments, and receive parking guidance.

Specialized Parking Services

AIRPORT AND METRO STATION PARKING: Managing large-scale parking operations for high-traffic areas with security and technology-driven solutions.

Commercial and Corporate Parking: Offering parking solutions tailored to commercial buildings and office complexes, ensuring smooth operations during peak hours.

Hospital and Healthcare Parking: Providing efficient parking services for healthcare facilities, focusing on accessibility and ease of use.

Consultancy and Design

PARKING LOT DESIGN AND PLANNING: Consulting on the optimal design and layout of parking spaces for new projects, including efficient use of space and traffic flow.

Feasibility Studies: Conducting research and analysis to determine the best parking solutions for a particular location based on traffic patterns and customer needs.

Safety and Security

24/7 SURVEILLANCE AND MONITORING: Installing CCTV cameras, security personnel, and monitoring systems for parking lots.

Fire Safety Compliance: Ensuring parking facilities comply with fire safety regulations and are equipped with necessary safety measures.

Access Control Systems: Providing secure entry and exit points using access cards, RFID, or LPR technology.
